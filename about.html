SKY DRIVE TRAVELS

Welcome to Sky Drive Travels, your trusted partner in safe, comfortable, and reliable transportation. Founded by Mr. Kamal Singh, we are proudly based in A-30, Sector 69, Noida, and have been serving schools, colleges, corporate groups, and luxury tour clients with dedication and care.

At Sky Drive Travels, we believe that every journey should be smooth, secure, and memorable. Our fleet of well-maintained buses and experienced drivers ensure that you reach your destination on time and in style. Whether it’s a daily school route or a weekend getaway, we tailor our services to meet your needs.

We also operate under the name Angand Bus Service, expanding our reach and capabilities to serve a wider range of clients across Delhi NCR and beyond. With both firms working in harmony, we’re able to offer flexible solutions for group travel, event transportation, and long-distance tours.
